How to use:

There are 3 buttons to interact with at the top of the page,
each with their own unique function:

  • Sound → Typeface reacts to Audio Input (Volume)

  • Mouse → Typeface moves with the Cursor Position on the page

  • Motion → Typeface tracks your Face Movement (Left, Right, Up + Down)
